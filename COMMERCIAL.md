## What the AGPL means for you

The AGPL is a strong copyleft license. You are free to:

- Use, study, modify, and distribute Linx for personal and non-commercial purposes.
- Contribute improvements back to the project.

However, if you incorporate Linx (or any modified version) into a **commercial product or service**, the AGPL requires you to release the **entire combined work** under the AGPL. This includes SaaS and hosted services that use Linx internally.

## Commercial License

If you want to use Linx in a commercial product or service **without** the AGPL's open-source requirements, you need a commercial license.

Commercial licenses are available for:

- Embedding Linx in proprietary software or appliances
- Offering Linx as part of a hosted or managed service
- Any use case where releasing your source code under AGPL is not acceptable
